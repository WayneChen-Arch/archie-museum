安遲美術館

歡迎來到安遲美術館，這是一位三歲小朋友創作集合。

展覽分為展廊與幕後兩個部分。

展廊，呈現安遲日常的繪畫與雕塑創作，這裡有他畫下的第一幅畫。他最愛的創作主題：下雨、颳風、彩虹。除了這些系列作品，還有他的第一幅人物肖像畫。

幕後，以照片與影片的方式，記錄這些作品從無到有的過程。分享創作背後的故事。

在這裡，希望你能感受到他創作的自由和熱情。若有想說的，歡迎在每幅畫的留言區寫下感想。願你有個好心情。

Welcome to Archie Art Museum, a collection of works by a three-year-old.

The exhibition has two parts: the Gallery and Behind the Scenes.

The Gallery presents Archie’s everyday paintings and sculptures, including his first drawing. His favorite subjects—rain, wind, and rainbows—run through these series, along with his first portrait.

Behind the Scenes records, through photos and video, how these works came into being, and shares the stories behind them.

We hope you feel the freedom and enthusiasm in his making. If you have something to say, leave a note under each work. Wishing you a good day.
